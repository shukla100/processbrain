## Company Overview

Tabby Finance is a B2B fintech offering business banking, treasury management,
and high-yield cash accounts for venture-backed startups. Clients are primarily
seed-to-Series-C startups that need a business checking account, idle cash
management, and basic spend controls without dealing with a traditional bank.
Tabby partners with a chartered bank to hold deposits and is not a bank itself.

---

## Org Structure

Sales/Partnerships and Customer Success roll up to the Head of Growth. Compliance
and Treasury Operations roll up to the Head of Risk & Operations. Engineering
rolls up to the CTO. Finance rolls up to the CFO. All department leads report
into the CEO.

## Known Pain Points

- Compliance risk review has no defined SLA — applications can sit for days
  with no escalation path
- No documented process yet for treasury cash management setup or ongoing
  account monitoring after onboarding completes

---

## Strategic Priorities

- Reduce time-to-first-deposit for new accounts
- Expand accepted business types beyond standard SaaS/tech startups

---

## Key Thresholds & Policies

- Standard KYB review applies to most applicants; manual compliance review is
  required for higher-risk industries (e.g. crypto-adjacent, money services
  businesses) regardless of company size
- Accounts cannot be provisioned in the ledger until compliance has issued a
  final approval — no exceptions
