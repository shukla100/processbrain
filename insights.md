**2026-06-16 — business-account-onboarding**

Compliance's risk review step has no defined SLA, and per leadership notes flagged
applications have sat for up to a week with no escalation trigger — this is likely
the single biggest drag on time-to-first-deposit, which is a stated company priority.
Suggested next step: define a 48-hour SLA for flagged reviews with an automatic
escalation to Marcus if unresolved, then track actual review time to confirm impact.
